## Temporal Trends in Colorectal Cancer Incidence

- **Topic:** Applied statistical analysis and cancer incidence trends
- **Output:** Journal article in *Cancer Control*
- **Methods and themes:** Temporal trend analysis, age-specific incidence, applied statistical reporting, reproducible analysis

This project studied colorectal cancer incidence and case numbers among individuals aged 45-49 in the United States during 2001-2019. The work was published in *Cancer Control*.

- [Publication page](/publication/GR)
- [Journal article](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/10732748251327715)

## Numerical Study of Kakeya Maximal Inequalities

- **Topic:** Mathematical analysis and numerical experimentation
- **Output:** Research poster and presentation
- **Methods and themes:** Numerical study, Kakeya-type problems, mathematical visualization, undergraduate research

This UW–Madison Mathematics project investigated Kakeya maximal inequalities through numerical study and visualization. The project resulted in a department presentation and poster.

- [Talk page](/talks/MXM2024)

## Free Central Limit Theorem and Dyck Paths

- **Topic:** Free probability, combinatorics, and random matrix theory
- **Output:** Directed Reading Program presentation
- **Methods and themes:** Mathematical exposition, noncommutative probability, semicircular law, combinatorial structures

This Directed Reading Program project studied the free central limit theorem, semicircular elements, and Dyck paths, with emphasis on the combinatorial viewpoint of free probability.

- [Talk page](/talks/DRP2024)
