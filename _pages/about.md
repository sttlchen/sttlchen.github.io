Hello!/Guten Tag!/你好！
======

I am a Master of Statistical Science student at Duke University. I graduated from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in December 2024 with a B.S. in Mathematics and Statistics. My academic interests lie at the intersection of statistical methodology, computation, and data-driven scientific applications.

I plan to pursue doctoral study in statistics, biostatistics, and related quantitative fields. I am especially interested in research that connects rigorous statistical methods with reproducible computation and applied scientific questions. I am also actively seeking TA/RA opportunities. You can contact me by <a href="mailto:tc434@duke.edu">email</a>, and my Duke department page is available <a href="https://scholars.duke.edu/person/Steven.Chen">here</a>.

## Research Interests

- Bayesian statistical modeling and probabilistic machine learning
- Causal inference and statistical methods for observational studies
- Statistical computing, reproducible workflows, and data analysis
- Applied biostatistics, epidemiology, and public health data science
- Probability, combinatorics, and mathematical foundations of statistics
